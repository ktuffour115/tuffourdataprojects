Velvet Bean Café Data Cleaning for Analytics

This was a standout project involving service data from various regions across the United Kingdom. The dataset was riddled with errors, missing values, inconsistencies, and incorrectly labeled regions. Using Excel functions such as IF, SUBSTITUTE, AND, OR, ISNUMBER, XLOOKUP, and more, we successfully cleaned and structured the data in preparation for migration to a centralized analytics platform. This addressed the café’s data quality challenges, enabling the effortless creation of clean pivot tables and reliable dashboards, ultimately enhancing their business reporting and data-driven decision-making.

Trendluxe Hub Sales Performance Analysis and Dashboard

This project involved analyzing a large retail dataset spanning multiple years, customer demographics, product categories, and sales regions. The raw data contained inconsistencies, duplicates, and unstructured values that required extensive cleaning and transformation. Using advanced Excel functions, alongside pivot tables and calculated fields, the dataset was standardized and modeled for analysis. A fully interactive dashboard was then developed, featuring drill-down capabilities by region, customer segment, and product category. This solution streamlined reporting processes, uncovered profit and loss trends, and delivered actionable insights into customer behavior and sales performance. The automation reduced manual reporting time by over 60%, while empowering business stakeholders to make faster, data-driven decisions.

Café Harmony Data Analysis and Dashboarding

This project focused on analyzing Café Harmony’s multi-year sales, customer demographics, inventory, and employee performance data to address operational inefficiencies and uncover growth opportunities. The raw dataset contained duplicates, blanks, and inconsistencies, which were cleaned and standardized using Excel functions (IF, XLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH, SUMIFS, and PivotTables) for structured analysis. Interactive dashboards and visual reports were built to track top-selling products, customer segments, seasonal sales trends, and stock utilization. Key insights revealed muffins and cappuccinos as star products, young adults as the highest-spending demographic, and critical gaps in inventory and staff performance. The findings drove actionable recommendations, including demand-based inventory planning, targeted promotions, and employee incentive programs. This data-driven solution streamlined operations, boosted forecasting accuracy, and empowered Café Harmony to improve customer satisfaction and sustain revenue growth.
